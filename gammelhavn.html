Gammelhavn

Heltslaette

Gammelhavn is a small port city on the northern coast of the Poisk Sea.
The capital city which makes of the majority of the land area of the Principality of Heltslaette, it is very important.
The tiny village of Gammelhavn des Noertdorts lies just outside of the city, though some consider it a part of Gammelhavn due to proximity.
