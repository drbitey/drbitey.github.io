Glashinol

Lorillal

Glashinol is a small village located in the northern plains of the Republic of Lorillal.
Despite its small size, Glashinol is currently and has historically been the capital of the large country of Lorillal.
In recent times, though, it has been considered to move the capital from Glashinol to the southern city of Havington.
