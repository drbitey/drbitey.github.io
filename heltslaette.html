The Principality of Heltslaette

Owned by Mike

The Principality of Heltslaette is a small kingdom straddling a small chunk of the northwestern coast of the Poisk Sea.
Ruled by a prince in the town of Gammelhavn, it identifies itself as a princly monarchy.
The government is currently in a state of vassalization under Mestrland, the only other country which it borders.
Only two main settlements exist in the country, Gammelhavn and Noertdort des Gammelhavns, the latter of which is sometimes considered a part of Gammelhavn due to proximity.
