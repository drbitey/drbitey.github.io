Vestal

Mestrland

Vestal is a medium-sized town and the capital of the Empire of Mestrland. (Rike til Mestrland)
It lies within the North Plains (Nordligeslette) and near the Vestal Forests.
Holding the only fragments of the lack of a centralized government in Mestrland, it is a nice town compared to the rest of Mestrland.
