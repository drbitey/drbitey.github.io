The Republic of Lorillal

Owned by Benneth

Lorillal, the shining gem of the Poisk Sea, is a highly developed nation with its capital based in the small northern village of Glashinol.
Even though Glashinol may be its capital, the southern city of Havington is the largest and most notable settlement in the country.
With its large navy and military, Lorillal protects the world whole.
