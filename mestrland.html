Rike til Mestrland

Owned by Benny

The Empire of Mestrland (Rike til Mestrland) is a pirate state encompassing a large portion of the northern coasts of the Poisk Sea.
Its geography can be easily described as harsh and freezing. In the west lay the Western Wastes, (Vestavfallen) a massive stretch of completely uninhabitable snow wastes.
The south and east areas of the country are the only inhabitable areas. Vestal lay by the Vestal Forest, while small fishing villages dot the southern coast.
Controlled by a pirate regime, the empire is constantly raiding and pillaging small settlements along the coasts of the sea on which it lies.
Currently the Principality of Heltslaette is in a state of vassalization under Mestrland due to previous pillages.
