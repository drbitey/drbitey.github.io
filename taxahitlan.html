Motoca-Xihuitl Tax'ahitlan

Owned by Mike

The Old Order of Tax'ahitlan (Motoca Xihuitl Tax'ahitlan) is an ancient group of loosely associated swamp tribes spread across the world.
While there is practically no centralized government, tribe leaders do commonly meet in the ancient temple of Teotlhuitz, making some believe it to be their "capital".
Very little remains of this once great confederation, as most ancient cities are now left in ruins after thousands of years of harsh swamp weather and human abandonment.
Due to their ancient nature, the Order does not associate itself with a flag or capital city.
